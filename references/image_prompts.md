# 文章配图提示词模板

本文件定义各类公众号文章的配图提示词模板，使用时根据选题内容填充。

## 封面图模板

### 科技热点类
```
Professional magazine cover image featuring {主题相关人物/元素}, {热点事件}, {年份} news style, clean corporate aesthetic, subtle AI circuit patterns in background, modern design, high quality, photorealistic, 16:9 aspect ratio
```

**示例：**
```
Professional magazine cover image featuring Tim Cook announcing retirement, Jon Ternus taking over Apple CEO position, 2026, clean corporate aesthetic, subtle AI circuit patterns in background, modern design
```

```
Motivational business illustration showing professional workers embracing AI technology, modern smart office, warm lighting, inspiring atmosphere, clean design, infographic style
```

## 提示词优化技巧

1. **使用具体描述**：避免模糊词汇，使用具体明确的名词和动词
2. **添加风格关键词**：photorealistic, illustration, infographic, magazine cover
3. **指定配色**：如 "blue and white color scheme"
4. **添加质量修饰**：high quality, professional, clean design
5. **指定构图**：centered, symmetrical, rule of thirds

## 禁忌事项

- 避免政治敏感内容
- 不使用真实车牌号、门牌号
- 不使用未授权的品牌logo
- 避免过于暴露或暗示性的图片
- 不使用真实儿童形象
